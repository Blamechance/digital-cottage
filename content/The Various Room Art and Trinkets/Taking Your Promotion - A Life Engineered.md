# Summary:
1. Make your intentions for a promotion crystal clear. 
2. Make an argument you deserve more scope. 
3. Don't postpone yourself -- start your promotion journey **now**. 


### 1. Make your intentions crystal clear. 
Communicate clearly to your manager:
> I want to get promoted, and I'd like to work with you to **understand where I'm at** and **what it would take** to make it happen."

If you Manager responds with either of the following attitudes — you should work with them to guide your promotion:
- They actively proceed to support and guide you. 
-  They have some concerns, but they are willing to work with you. 

However, if they respond with:
- Deflection or postponing the conversation.

This is a sign you will need to make some active decisions regarding your plans. 

### 2. Make an argument you deserve more scope. 
- It's not about doing better at your **current** scope, but about being ready for **additional** scope. 
- Remember that **taking your promotion** means growing in areas you feel uncomfortable.
	- If you were already there and had the skills you needed, then you would already be at the next level. 
- Use the levelling criteria's available, and go over it with your manager, to explore where your gaps may be. 
	- Recognise you may have gaps, rather than doing better at things you are already good at. 

### 3. Don't postpone yourself -- start your promotion journey **now**. 
- Don't let other people tell you to wait, to postpone yourself and simply focus on "being a better engineer", where the promotion will come after. 
	- They are not mutually exclusive -- you can become a better engineer, while also focusing on active promotion focuses. 
- The cost of waiting for a promotion only goes up.
	- The requirements don't get easier. 
	- Uncertain factors outside of your control, are not guaranteed to get easier to manage.  
	- The competition will only increase. 
	- Your previous achievements will fade from view due to unfortunate recency bias -- momentum is should be capitalised on. 
- Perfect timing does not exist.
